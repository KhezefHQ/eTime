Watch Repairs

Batteries, Straps
& Full Servicing

Whether your watch has stopped, needs a new strap, or requires a full service, we have the tools and the expertise to get it right. We work on all makes and models, from everyday fashion watches to precision Swiss timepieces.

We carry a wide selection of watch batteries and straps in-store. If we don't have the exact part or strap you need, we'll order it for you, as we want your watch back on your wrist as quickly as possible.

  • Watch battery replacements for all sizes and brands
  • Strap and bracelet changes: leather, metal, and rubber
  • Crystal (glass) replacement
  • Full watch servicing & movement cleaning
  • Can't find the part? We'll order it for you

Jewellery Repairs

Restore Your
Treasured Pieces

A broken clasp, a loose stone, a ring that no longer fits; these things happen. Our skilled craftspeople repair jewellery of all kinds, from everyday silver pieces to precious gold heirlooms. We treat every item with the care it deserves.

Bring us your worn or damaged jewellery and we'll assess the repair honestly and give you a fair quote. Most repairs are completed in the workshop with quick turnaround times, depending on the complexity, but we will communicate that to our customers.

  • Ring resizing, made larger or smaller
  • Chain repairs and soldering
  • Clasp replacements
  • Stone re-setting & tightening
  • General polishing & restoration
  • Silver & gold jewellery repairs

Antique Watches

Restoration, Buying
& Selling

Antique and vintage timepieces are our passion. Whether you have a precious heirloom that has stopped running, or you're looking to buy or sell a classic timepiece, our specialists have the knowledge and experience to help.

  • Antique movement servicing & cleaning
  • Pocket watch repairs & restoration
  • Vintage wristwatch overhauls

  • Antique and vintage watches for sale
  • Advice & valuation on old timepieces
