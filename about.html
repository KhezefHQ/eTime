Our Story

About eTime

A family-run jewellery and watch shop in Wynberg, trusted by our community for over 26 years.

Since 2000

26 Years at the Heart
of Wynberg

eTime Watch & Jewellery opened its doors in 2000 at Shop 2B, Maynard Mall in Wynberg, Cape Town. What started as a small, passionate venture has grown, year by year, customer by customer, into one of the area's most trusted and beloved jewellery destinations.

We are a proudly family-run business. That means every piece we sell, every repair we complete, and every custom ring we craft carries the personal attention and genuine care that only a family business can offer. We know our customers by name. We celebrate their engagements, anniversaries, and milestones with them.

Over the past 26 years, we have built our reputation on three simple principles: quality, honesty, and lasting relationships. Whether you walk in for a watch battery or a bespoke diamond engagement ring, you'll leave knowing you were treated fairly and with skill.

Who We Are

What Makes Us Different

These aren't just words on a wall; they're how we've run our shop for over two decades.

Trustworthy

We've earned the trust of the Wynberg community over 26 years by always being honest: about prices, about repairs, and about what your pieces are truly worth.

Family-Run

We're not a franchise or a chain. We are a real family with a real shop, and we treat every customer the way we'd want our own family to be treated.

Expert Craftsmanship

From delicate watch repairs to fully custom engagement rings, every piece gets the care and skill it deserves. We don't cut corners, ever.

Accessible Prices

Beautiful jewellery shouldn't be out of reach. Our silver collection is priced fairly, and for gold pieces, we work with you on a deposit arrangement that suits your budget.

Community Rooted

We've watched children grow up, celebrated weddings, and comforted losses, all from our little shop in Maynard Mall. Wynberg is our home, and our customers are our neighbours.

Passion for Excellence

Jewellery and watches aren't just inventory to us; they're art, craft, and memory. We take pride in everything that leaves our shop, big or small.

Come Say Hello

We'd love to meet you. Whether you're looking for a special gift, need a repair, or want to design a custom piece, pop into our shop in Wynberg or drop us a WhatsApp.
