Our Story

A Business
Built on Trust

eTime Watch & Jewellery has called Maynard Mall, Wynberg home since 2000. For 26 years we have served the Wynberg community and greater Cape Town with honesty, warmth, and genuine expertise, treating every customer with the warmth and care you'd only get from a real, independent local business.

We carry beautiful silver jewellery at accessible prices, and can source gold on request. Our team are watch specialists, from fitting a battery or new strap to overhauling a precious antique timepiece. Whatever you need, we've seen it and we know how to handle it with care.
